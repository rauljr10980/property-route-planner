# How to Find "ADD KEY" Button

## You're on the Service Account Page - Here's How to Create a Key:

### Option 1: Look for a Tab at the Top

The page should have **tabs** at the top, like:
- **Details** (currently selected)
- **Permissions**
- **Keys** ← **Click this tab!**

1. **Look at the top of the page** - you should see tabs
2. **Click on the "Keys" tab**
3. You'll see the "ADD KEY" button there

---

### Option 2: Look for "+ ADD KEY" Button Directly

1. **Look at the top-right corner** of the page
2. You should see a button like:
   - **"+ ADD KEY"**
   - **"Add Key"**
   - Or a **"+"** icon with "Add key"

3. **Click it**

---

### Option 3: Look for Menu/Actions

1. **Look for "Actions" column** (if visible in the table)
2. Or look for a **"⋮" (three dots)** menu
3. Click it and look for "Create key" or "Add key"

---

### Option 4: Use the Page Menu Bar

At the very top of the page, look for:
```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  Service Accounts  │  [Permissions] [Keys]  │
│                    │  [+ CREATE SERVICE...]  │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

Click on the **"Keys"** section or button.

---

## Most Likely: Click "Keys" Tab

**The most common location is in a tab at the top of the page.**

1. **Look at the top** where you see service account info
2. You should see tabs like: **Details | Permissions | Keys**
3. **Click "Keys"**
4. Then you'll see **"+ ADD KEY"** button
